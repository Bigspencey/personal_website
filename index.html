Recent Projects

NoodleSkoodle

Developed and deployed a platform that aggregates over 1400 free online course from organizations like Udemy, Coursera, and Edx. NoodleSkoodle gives users the power to track and take ownership of their online education by unifying these disparate providers.

NFL Home Field Advantage

A D3.js map of the United States designed to represent each NFL teams' Home Field Advantage over the past decade. Composite values for the project were derived from a teams' average offense/defense and win percentage every season.
